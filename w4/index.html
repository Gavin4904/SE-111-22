About The Movie Office Space

Peter Gibbons who hates his job at a software company Initech is left in a mediative state after an incident with a hypnotherapist. He takes this attitude into his office job. When he and his friends learns that they will be victims of a downsizing that hatch a plot to ruin the soulless company they work for. There actions lead to the downfall of Initech.

Director:Mike Judge
