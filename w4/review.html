Review Of The Movie Office Space

Gavin Stoddard

February 2,2023


★★★★★

The 1999 movie Office Space has to be one of my favourites of all time. I remember watching this movie at a young age and not understanding most of the humor but now that I am older I think that it is much more funny. This movie also has am amazing sound track featuring songs from Ice Cube and (my favorite 90's rap group) Geto Boys. It is hilarious to watch Peter snap and unintentionally make his life better in the process. Overall this is a great movie and I highly recommend it to anyone looking for a good laugh.

Office Space Movie Trivia

  • Milton's iconic red stapler was custom red by the props department. After the film came out the Swingline brand had so many request for the red stapler that they started to offer it again.
  • In front of Initech there is a sculpture of a square peg in a round hole.This is based off of the idiomatic expression.
  • The fictional restarant Chotchkie's seen in the film actually takes place in the real life restarant called "The Alligator Grille"
